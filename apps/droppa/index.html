The Vault Process
1
Submit intake form + pay $9
Fill in card details, declare value, pre-pay optional extra storage. USDC approved on-chain. You receive a unique Vault ID.
2
Mail your card with Vault ID
Use rigid top loader + padded envelope. Write your Vault ID on the outside. Use tracked shipping — we recommend USPS Priority or UPS.
3
We receive, verify, and digitize
Card is inspected, photographed (front/back, high-res), cert number verified against grading service database. Physical card stored in climate-controlled vault.
4
DCV NFT minted to your wallet
ERC-721 NFT on RAWNet with scan metadata on IPFS. The NFT IS the card — sell it on Droppa marketplace, transfer to any wallet, or use as collateral.
5
Sell or redeem anytime
Sell the NFT on Droppa — whoever holds it owns the physical card. Or burn the NFT ($19 fee) and we mail you the card, fully insured.
