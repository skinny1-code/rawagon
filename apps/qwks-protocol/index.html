AIPolicyRouter — Route Intent
The AIPolicyRouter is an AI-governed token routing system. An AI policy signer generates off-chain RouteIntent structs, signs them with EIP-712, and the contract verifies + executes the route. Prevents replay attacks via nonce + deadline.
